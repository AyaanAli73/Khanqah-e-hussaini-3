Guidance & Answers

Royal Decrees

Q1

Is the Khanqah open for ladies?

Yes, there is a separate designated area (Pardah arrangement) for ladies. We request all visitors to maintain a modest dress code and observe silence within the spiritual premises to maintain the sanctity of the place.

Q2

Is Token Booking Free?

Absolutely. The token booking service is completely Free of Charge. It is a system designed to manage the crowd and save your valuable time. Please beware of anyone asking for money for appointments.

Q3

Can non-Muslims visit?

Khanqah-e-Hussaini is a place of humanity first. We welcome brothers and sisters from all faiths (Hindu, Sikh, Christian, Jain) with open hearts. The message of Sufism is universal love, peace, and brotherhood.

Q4

Is parking available?

Yes, there is ample parking space available near the Khanqah for both two-wheelers and four-wheelers. Volunteers will guide you to the designated spots upon arrival.
